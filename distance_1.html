Distance Learning Program on Process Equipment & Pressure Vessel Technology


Course Content:

  • Process equipment & pressure Vessel Technology
  • Introduction about process industries
  • List of equipment used in industries, Their introduction applications
  • Indian process plants, fabricators, engineering companies, consultants
  • Basic engineering knowledge on engineering materials, Strength of materials
  • Introduction of standards and other codes related to pressure vessel & Process equipments (ASME Sec VIII Div 1, Sec II, Sec IX, IS 2825, TEMA API650 etc)
  • Design and analysis of Pressure Vessel & Components
  • Design & Analysis of storage Tanks
  • Selection of materials for process equipments
  • Fabrication of process equipments, plate forming, cutting, welding
  • Exposure to Pressure Vessel Design Software
  • Local Stress Analysis at Nozzle Vessel junction software and others problem solving sessions
  • Inspection & Testing

Who Should Attend?


This program is useful for fresh & experienced engineers or engineers desiring to shift to Process Equipment and Pressure Vessel engineering disciplines. The course is continuously revised for the updated technology.
