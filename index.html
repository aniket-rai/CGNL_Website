Charisma Global Networks Limited


To assist the organizations and individuals in identifying the training needs and requirements, fulfilling the same and synergizing the efforts of human resource development in a systematic way
This is the philosophy at CGNL, a group incorporated in New Zealand. That is aptly reflected in our motto Partnering Success.

In the 21st century organization, every aspect of managing organizations has become dynamic except Human Resource requirements. Updation and upgradation of skills has become very important with the constant developments in the fields of engineering, management and business. With this perspective, CGNL launched its first course in the area of Piping Technology.
