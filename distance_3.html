Distance Learning Program on Piping Technology


Course Content:

  • Introduction about process industries world wide
  • List of piping components used in industries , their introduction, applications
  • Local process plants, fabrication, engineering companies, consultants and site erection contractors
  • Understanding of basic engineering, engineering materials, strength of materials, fluid mechanics
  • Understanding of process flow, P&I diagrams, Interpretations, Plot plant & equipment layout generation
  • Introduction to ASME B31.3, B31.4, B31.8 and other codes related to process piping and pipelines
  • Selection of piping components (Flanges, Fittings, Valves, Supports, Expansion joint selection & application of Pumps)
  • Selection and application of compressor
  • Piping plan, pipe sizing, development of isometric drawings
  • Flow and pressure drop calculations
  • Selection on materials for process piping components
  • Stress analysis, flexibility stress analysis
  • Fabrication, including welding, particularly for piping components
  • Introduction to piping flow and pressure drop evaluation & stress analysis Software
  • Inspection & quality control
  • Quality control and inspection for raw materials, components and welding
  • Inspection & testing for piping system
  • Non destructive Examination of piping

Who Should Attend?


This program is useful for fresh & experienced engineers or engineers desiring to shift to Process Equipment and Pressure Vessel engineering disciplines. The course is continuously revised for the updated technology.
