Distance Learning Program on Piping Systems and Pipeline Integrity of Oil, Gas and Process Industries


Course Content:

  • Piping & Pipeline Terminology, Components
  • Piping & pipeline design standards, ASME B31.1, B31.3, B31.4, B31.8
  • Pipeline Design, Construction & Maintenance
  • Materials, Welding Processes
  • Weld Discontinuities
  • Corrosion principles, types, Inspection
  • Discussion on API 570 (Piping & pipeline Inspection, repairs, alteration and re-rating)
  • Inspection Interval
  • Corrosion Rate evaluation
  • Minimum Safe Thickness evaluation
  • API 579 (Fitness-for-Service) Analysis
  • Pipe Line Integrity Management and Strategies
  • Main factors affecting pipeline integrity
  • Pressure and Leak Testing, Hydro test
  • External and internal Protection, Cathodic Protection

Who Should Attend?


This program is useful for fresh & experienced engineers or engineers desiring to shift to Process Equipment and Pressure Vessel engineering disciplines. The course is continuously revised for the updated technology.
